Questions

How does my team get set up?
Pick your tier and pay online. Your team is created automatically — you'll get an email with your team ID and coach login. Log in and you're ready to add your roster the same day.
How do players join?
You add them from your coach dashboard and they're linked to your team ID automatically — no codes for players to type in.
Which tier do I need?
Youth/Flag/7v7 for youth organizations, Middle School for MS programs, and High School/Org for high school programs and larger organizations.
Can we see it first?
Absolutely — book a demo and we'll walk your staff through it before you buy.
Is there training?
Yes. Once you're set up we reach out to help you get your playbook loaded and your team rolling.
